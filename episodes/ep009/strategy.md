# Strategy — ep009: No Blockers, Hockey Stick Queue

## Brief

The AI standup bot posts "no blockers" every day while the incident queue graphs a perfect hockey stick.

## Angle

Deadpan contrast between automated morale theater (Slack standup summaries, calendar hygiene) and the incident curve that does not care about your green status. The joke is alignment: everyone is following process while the graph goes vertical.

## Format

- **Length:** ~38–42 seconds
- **Pacing:** Fireship-adjacent cuts; 10–11 scenes; mockups carry the bureaucratic texture; one b-roll beat for scale / stress / data vibe
- **Opening:** Card hook naming the contradiction
- **Button:** LGTM card

## Scene flow (creative)

1. Card — hook: bot mandate vs reality
2. Kinetic — repetition / "no blockers" beat
3. Slack mockup — bot transcript
4. Kinetic — queue inflection
5. JIRA mockup — incident ticket bloat
6. Kinetic — hockey stick label
7. Calendar mockup — standup block survives green bot
8. B-roll — stock: charts, stress, or office tension (query tuned for Giphy/Pixabay)
9. Code mockup — trivial cron / automation
10. Kinetic — synthesis line
11. Card — LGTM

## B-roll search direction

Primary query: business analytics graph trending up, or stressed engineer at monitors — generic enough for stock APIs. Fallback card if clip fails: headline about the curve vs the standup.

## Tone

Mike Judge meets Gary Larson. Safety-manual delivery. No winking.

## Risks

- Over-long VO in mockups — keep lines declarative; storyboard can trim duration hints if TTS runs long in pipeline after approval.
