# Audio Direction

## Music

Low, restrained electronic bed. Keep volume at `0.10`. It should feel like compliance training for a repository with commit access.

## SFX

Use small mechanical punctuation:

- `thud` for thesis and final button.
- `typewriter` under code and Slack mockups.
- `pop` for mockup entrances.
- `stinger` for the six-month pivot.
- `ding` for the fake calendar approval process.

## Mix Notes

Do not make the SFX cute. The VO should sound like a process document being read by someone who has already approved the budget.
